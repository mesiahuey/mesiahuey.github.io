bio

"Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up."

-Pablo Picasso

Mesia Huey was born in Boulder, Colorado and grew up in different parts of the Midwest on Sunday newspaper comics, picture books and animated movies. As a child, she was happy if she had just crayons and paper, and today, she still loves bold, saturated colors. Her favorite place to go was the library, especially for books with images of different animals she could draw and read about.

She received her Bachelor of Arts in Illustration from Brigham Young University--Idaho in 2012. Shortly after graduation, she married Bryce Huey, who has become a great source of inspiration and imagination in her work, and he's been very encouraging to her in pursuing a career in Illustration.

In the past, she has created illustrations for a keynote presentation of the 2006 GIS/CAMA Conference at Disney World in Orlando, Florida, which the Director of Sales and Marketing described as, "the best presentation [he'd] ever seen." In her work, she hopes to inspire children with books to lead them into finding their own dreams to pursue, as she found her love of art in reading.
